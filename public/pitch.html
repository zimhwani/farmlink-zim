The Problem

No price discovery

Farmers sell to the first buyer who arrives, with no knowledge of market prices. Middlemen capture 40–60% of value.

No market access

70% of smallholder farmers in Zimbabwe have no digital channel to reach buyers outside their immediate area.

🌧️

No advisory access

AGRITEX has 1 extension officer per 1,000+ farmers. Most smallholders have never received professional agronomic advice.
